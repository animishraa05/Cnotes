## Types of knowledge

*declerative knowledge* 
Kind of knowledge that specifies what but does not specify how in it , like **chatgpt stores the facts in declerative knowledge in it database**
- e.g., Akshita is ustad
*procedural knowledge*
Kind of Knowledge that represents and specifies how to do something as in procedure or as in algorithm way which is not a fact 
- helps *Ai* in performing actions
- also helps in creating robotic systems.

*meta knowledge*
Kind of knowledge that tell about knowledge meaning AI gets awareness about its own knowledge
- this is used in intelligent system.
- it helps ai in organizing its knowledge.

*heuristic knowledge*
kind of knowledge in which you guess by your expertness in the domain.
- helps ai to find fast solutions in certain domains.

*Structural Knowledge*
kind of knowledge which defines how to solve problem in structured way. 
tells how concepts and objects are connected to each other.
 - like it is used in making semantic webs, and also helps in restoring the info in organised way.
in one line 

- Declarative → Facts (Kya hai?)
    
- Procedural → Steps (Kaise karna hai?)
    
- Meta → Knowledge ke baare mein knowledge
    
- Heuristic → Experience-based shortcuts
    
- Structural → Relations between concepts
